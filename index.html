What We Do?

We are an independent music publication that focuses on upcoming and talented artists within the wider music industry. We Strive to bring change to the industry through sharing stories and highlighting the journeys behind your favourite music!

We work with a spectrum of artists from all over the music industry, bringing you news, interviews, and giving you tips on everything you need to know!

Our photographers and writers work hard to capture these integral moments.
