South-of-Salem @ Roxoff Festival 04.05.2025

We Caught Up with South-of-Salem at Roxoff Festival ahead of their appearance at BloodStock Festival 2025! We had our Photographer, Emily Shinn capture the last half of the festival at the Brickmakers in Norwich On the 4th May 2025

“ROXOFF is an action packed indoor festival at the Brickmakers in Norwich, which started at 14:30 and didn’t end until gone 23:00; with the crowd never wavering. “

We managed to catch Muddibrooke performing just before South-of-Salem:

”With South of Salem appearing at around 22:00 to a packed room cheering their name and awaiting their arrival. “

“Performing banger after banger never slowing up so they can fit in their whole set before the dreaded 11pm curfew, the fans enjoyed every last second of the music, with the energetic crowd singing along to their every word and not wanting the show to end, a packed room for the band.”
