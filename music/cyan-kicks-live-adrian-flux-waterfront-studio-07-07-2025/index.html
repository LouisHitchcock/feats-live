Cyan Kicks Live @ Adrian Flux Waterfront Studio 07.07.2025

It may have been a Monday night but you wouldn’t have known that with the electric energy Cyan Kicks brought to their show.

Their bold stage presence bringing everyone in the room together; and strong lighting creating a perfect atmosphere on stage that was hard to look away from, the entire room felt like an energised bubble. Belting out song after song never slowing and making sure the crowd danced and joined in with them so everyone felt the music just the same.

With each member of the band dressed uniquely yet all connected somehow to form almost a uniform, it made you feel as if you were part of a huge stage production on a smaller scale, allowing us to see just how much care and effort the band feels for their performance, truly an intimate moment to acknowledge between the fans and the band.
