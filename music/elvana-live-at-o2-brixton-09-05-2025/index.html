Elvana Live @ O2 Brixton 10.05.2025

Elvana is a uk based Elvis fronted nirvana tribute band from Newcastle -upon- Tyne. Having already rocked multiple festivals including download and Glastonbury  they returned to London to take on the mighty O2 Academy Brixton.

Explosive opening with confetti and energy. nirvana classics such as; Lithium and Smells Like Teen Spirit blended with Elvis old favourites such as; Hound dog, Blue Sued Shoes and A little less conversation. Rocked through the atmosphere with all heads banging.

When the final notes rang out, the floor was still bouncing and the energy in the room hung in the air like static. From the front barrier to the back of the balcony, the crowd had been on their feet all night, singing, dancing, and throwing themselves into every moment. As fans slowly filtered out into the Brixton night, confetti stuck to their shoes and wide smiles on their faces, the unmistakable sound of Elvis-meets-Nirvana mashups was still ringing in everyone’s ears—marking the end of a night that won’t be forgotten any time soon.
