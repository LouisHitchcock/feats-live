Hotwax Talk Debut Album & Nova Twins Tour before their Latitude 2025 Set

[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="1200"] Tom Ellis (TJEphotos/FEATS) [/caption]

We caught up with Hotwax ahead of their Latitude 2025 set — fresh from a weekend in Germany and ready to hit the Trailer Park stage. They told us about the creative roots they’ve built in Hastings and Brighton, the buzz around their debut album earlier this year, and how their latest single brings a new flavour to their sound.

We also chatted about their upcoming autumn tour with Nova Twins — a full-circle moment for the band, having known them since their early teens — plus hints at a headline run and new music on the horizon.
