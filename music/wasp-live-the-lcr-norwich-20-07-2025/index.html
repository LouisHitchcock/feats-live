W.A.S.P Live @ The LCR Norwich 20.07.2025

W.A.S.P came and the put on one strong show and brought the fans with them to prove it. You already know you’re going to be in for a treat when most of the fans have been seeing the band for 30+ years. A long standing career and dedicated fan base say a lot about a performer, and it definitely proved right for W.A.S.P. 

The crowd was eager and ready to go from the minute doors opened, with the crowd getting involved and enjoying the support band Tailgunner and their electrifying set, with every member running around the stage during the performance; a real band to catch, with their songs matching their energy, it was hard to not feel like you wanted to join in. With a pre-warning for no crowd surfing before W.A.S.P arrived on stage, you really felt the energy from the audience and the need for commotion and perhaps a mosh pit or two. 

W.A.S.P’s stage set up certainly caught your eye, with various cloth posters adorning the back of the stage with slogans and images, as well as a mic stand in the shape of a curved spine, adorned with a skull and crossbones in front of the mic itself. 

Once they started playing you felt the energy in the room shift, with everyone feeling the music pulse through you, as the stage was seemingly taken over with such a presence you almost couldn’t do anything but marvel at them. 

Although thankfully not witnessing any of their antics with raw meat being thrown around like they used to, it’s safe to say W.A.S.P have still got it.
