Melin Meylin Live @ Norwich Arts Centre 09.05.2025

The night started off at 8pm with a set from the wonderful and charming welsh singer Bitw who captivated the audience all by himself, and quickly had people coming in to listen.


Whilst the stage was being setup for Melin Melyn many people flocked to the bar to get more refreshments ready for the main act, however those who stayed got to see the set unfold, including a life sized stuffed doll wearing a pink boiler suit and glasses to match the band.

Once Melin Melyn arrived they captured the audiences attention from the start, coming on stage and saluting everyone before starting the gig off. Their captivating energy allowing them to get strong audience engagement, including getting everyone to bark like a dog mid way through a song! With the set only pausing in between songs in order to talk about the importance of funding grassroots venues and supporting the arts.
